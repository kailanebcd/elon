Crucible Moment: The Falcon 1 Failures

SpaceX's first three rocket launches failed spectacularly, nearly killing the company. Instead of abandoning the approach, Musk insisted on understanding exactly why each failure occurred and implementing specific fixes. The fourth launch succeeded, saving SpaceX. Expensive failures are only worthless if you don't extract every possible lesson. Make failures data rather than disasters.

"When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor. This is about Mission Beyond Money. Your conviction is your greatest asset."
